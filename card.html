Polar Bear

"I can swim up to 6 miles per hour. To put that in perspective, Michael Phelps clocks in around 3.92 miles per hour."

  • Scientific Name: Ursus Maritimus
  • Average Weight: 770-1540 lbs
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Habitat: Arctic

The polar bear is a large bear that typically resides within the Arctic Circle. It's current conservation status is classified as vulnerable as it has been hunted extensively in the past.
